# Design Context

## Product
- Internal receipt replacement web app for generating a Thai reimbursement form on mobile-sized preview and PDF.

## Target Audience
- Internal staff preparing reimbursement paperwork quickly from a phone or desktop browser.

## Use Cases
- Fill expense rows
- Preview the printable form
- Export a PDF for submission

## Brand Personality
- Clear
- Practical
- Trustworthy

## UI Direction
- Keep the current lightweight app-like layout.
- Prioritize readability, touch-friendly controls, and print accuracy over visual experimentation.

## Assumptions
- This is an internal utility, so preserving the current visual style is more important than introducing a new design language.
